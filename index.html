NETEDGE TELECOMMUNICATION CHURN ANALYSIS

In a competitive telecommunications industry, our client faced significant challenges with customer churn, affecting their £16M annual revenue and market position. With a churn rate of 26.54%, we identified that customers with less than 1 year of subscription, month-to-month contracts, and those using electronic checks were at the highest risk. Fiber optic users (43.96%) and those opting for paperless billing (59.22%) also showed elevated churn rates. We recommended incentivizing long-term contracts with discounts and added services to reduce churn among month-to-month subscribers. Enhancing onboarding and customer support for new customers, who had the highest churn rate, was crucial. Promoting more secure payment methods like credit cards, which have lower churn rates than electronic checks, was also advised. Additionally, improving the quality of Fiber optic services and reevaluating the paperless billing process were key strategies. By implementing these data-driven strategies, the company aimed to significantly reduce churn, enhance customer retention, and increase their £16M annual revenue, demonstrating the power of targeted solutions in addressing business challenges and ensuring sustainable growth.

E-commerce Performance Analysis

Our e-commerce platform demonstrates robust performance with a total revenue of R$ 16.01 million from 6,999 orders, facilitated by 3,094 active sellers. The platform has garnered an average rating of 3.81, reflecting customer satisfaction, and hosts 99.44K customers with an average order value of R$ 154.10. The majority of transactions are conducted via credit cards, contributing R$ 13M to our revenue. Boleto payments follow with R$ 3M, while other payment methods like vouchers and debit cards show low contributions. Generally, Our seller and order counts peak in September and August, respectively, indicating heightened activity during these months. This analysis highlights the platform's strong market presence, particularly through credit card transactions, and suggests potential seasonality in sales patterns. These insights are crucial for strategic planning and enhancing the platform's performance year-round.
